### Employee Relations
**Q:** What is the primary purpose of an employee handbook?
**A:** To communicate company policies, procedures, and expectations to employees, providing a consistent framework for handling workplace issues.

---

### Talent Acquisition
**Q:** What is the difference between a job description and a job specification?
**A:** A job description outlines the duties and responsibilities of a role. A job specification outlines the qualifications, skills, and experience required to perform that role.

---

### Total Rewards
**Q:** Define "compra-ratio" and explain its significance in compensation.
**A:** Compa-ratio is the ratio of an employee's actual salary to the midpoint of their salary range. It is significant because it helps assess whether employees are being paid fairly internally and competitively externally.

---

### Learning & Development
**Q:** What are the four levels of the Kirkpatrick Evaluation Model?
**A:** 1. Reaction (Did they like it?) 2. Learning (Did they learn it?) 3. Behavior (Did they apply it?) 4. Results (Did it impact the business?)
